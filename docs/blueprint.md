# **App Name**: Forwarty Digital

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Header: Fixed header with logo, animated menu (Inicio, Acerca de, Servicios, Blog, Noticias, Contacto), demo button, and glassmorphism effect on scroll.
- Futuristic Hero Section: Dynamic background with moving gradients, animated title ('Transformamos la logística global con software inteligente'), subtitle, call-to-action buttons ('Explora nuestras soluciones' and 'Ver demo'), floating entrance effect, and futuristic 3D illustration of data flow.
- Interactive Service Cards: Interactive cards showcasing services like ERP Cloud Logístico, Automatización de Procesos, Integraciones y APIs, Consultoría en Transformación Digital, Inteligencia de Datos Logísticos, with hover animations and parallax background.
- Animated Blog & News Section: Blog and news articles divided into tabs/carousel, featuring image, category, title, summary, and date with hover and cascade entry effects.
- Contact Form with Validation: Contact form with name, email, company, message fields with validation, social media icons, and a gradient background with animation on scroll.
- Dynamic Footer: Dark footer with illuminated upper border, sitemap, quick links, copyright with dynamic year, and a final tagline.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#143466) for trust and technology.
- Background color: Desaturated dark blue (#224477) to maintain a technological and reliable feel.
- Accent color: Turquoise (#00A2AD) for CTAs, emphasizing innovation; its brightness and saturation differ markedly from the primary, to offer good contrast.
- Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines and short body text; its modern, computerized look fits well with the digital focus.
- Use minimalist icons for services and contact information.
- Responsive layout optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile, using reusable and modular components.
- Use Framer Motion for smooth transitions, scroll animations, and hover interactions to add a modern and engaging feel.
